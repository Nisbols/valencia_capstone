Semester Programs - How to Apply

The process to research, plan, and prepare for a semester study abroad program is a long one, so we recommend that you start at least one year prior to your program start date and no less than 6 months prior.

×
IT IS VERY IMPORTANT THAT YOU SELECT A PROGRAM THAT WILL PROVIDE YOU WITH A TRANSCRIPT ISSUED BY A U.S. INSTITUTION TO OBTAIN TRANSFER CREDIT. IF YOU DIRECTLY ENROLL IN AN OVERSEAS INSTITUTION THAT HAS NO U.S. AFFILIATION, YOU CANNOT USE YOUR FINANCIAL AID, YOU CANNOT GET A SAGE SCHOLARSHIP, YOU WILL NEED TO PAY FOR A NACES TRANSCRIPT EVALUATION UPON PROGRAM COMPLETION, AND THERE IS NO GUARANTEE THAT THE CREDITS WILL TRANSFER BACK TO VALENCIA.

How to Apply

  1. Contact the SAGE office at 407-582-3188 or studyabroad@valenciacollege.edu. Let us know what your plans are and be sure to check in along the way.
  2. REQUIRED! Print out and follow the instructions on the Semester Study Abroad Planner and use the checklist to help you navigate this process.
  3. Search for a program provider.
  4. Print out the following forms which have all been referenced in the Semester Study Abroad Planner above.
    • Budget Worksheet for Study Abroad
    • Consortium Agreement Form for Study Abroad
    • SAGE Scholarship Instructions and Application Formz
    • Recommended Packing List
    • Property Document Form
  • Study Abroad Scholarships
  • Passport (your passport should be valid for up to 90 days after your program end date)
  • Visa (verify if you need a visa and apply 45-120 days prior)
  • Medical/trip insurance
  • International Student ID Card

BEFORE YOU TRAVEL YOU MUST REGISTER WITH THE SAGE OFFICE 30 DAYS IN ADVANCE:
