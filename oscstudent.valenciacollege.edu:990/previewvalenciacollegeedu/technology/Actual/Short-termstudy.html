Valencia College offers a number of faculty-led, short-term study abroad programs for students. See the PROGRAMS links on the left navigation to view the current program offerings. Programs for 2017 are now open! Students should verify with a study abroad academic advisor to determine how participation in this study abroad program will affect their overall financial aid package. Also, courses must fit within the student's education plan in order for financial aid to cover it. STUDY ABROAD ADVISORS
