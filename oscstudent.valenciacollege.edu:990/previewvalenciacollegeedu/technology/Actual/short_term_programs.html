Short-Term Programs - 2018

Summer Applications are Open!

The programs listed below are part of a Valencia academic course. These short-term programs are led by Valencia faculty members, are one to two weeks long, and held over spring or summer. For most programs, participants must be a Valencia student although some allow transient and noncredit students to enroll as audit students. Please note that some programs may have prerequisites and all participants must be 18 years of age or older. See the How to Apply to Short-Term Programs for more information.

If you want to be considered for a program scholarship, you must be a degree-seeking student at Valencia, have a minimum GPA of 2.5, have no disciplinary or academic holds, and be registered in at least 3 credits for the term.

Please check the Sage Program Booklet in case you have questions about program finances, healthcare on study abroad, safety and emergency preparedness, etc.
